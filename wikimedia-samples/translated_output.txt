Patrick Wayne Swayze (/sweytiz/ SWAY-zee; August 18, 1952 - September 14, 2009) yali muzannyi wa firimu, omuyimbi, omuwandiisi w'ennyimba era omuzinyi Omumerika. Amanyiddwa nnyo olw'omukwano, obunkenke, n'okusaaga mu firimu z'ekika kya cult, Swayze yasunsulwa okuwangula engule za Golden Globes ssatu era yafuna emmunyeenye ku Hollywood Walk of Fame mu 1997. Swayze yafuna okusiimibwa olw'okuzannya firimu ya katemba The Outsiders (1983), firimu y'ebikolwa Red Dawn (1984), ne miniseries North and South (1985-1986). Okuyitamu kwe kwajja ne firimu y'omukwano eya Dirty Dancing (1987), eyafuna okusunsulwa kwa Golden Globe. Yayongera okumanyika mu firimu z'ebikolwa eza Road House (1989 ne Point Break (1991), era n'afuna okusunsulwa kwa Golden Globe emirundi emirala ebiri olw'emirimu gye mu firimu y'omukwano ey'enjawulo eya Ghost (1990) ne firimu ya road comedy To Wong Foo, Thanks for Everything! Julie Newmar (1995). Era yazannya mu firimu ya cult thriller Donnie Darko (2001). Ng'oggyeeko okuzannya emizannyo, Swayze yawandiika era n'akwata oluyimba "She's Like the Wind" olwa Dirty Dancing soundtrack album, olwatuuka ku nnamba ssatu ku billboard Hot 100. Era yasiimibwa olw'ekifaananyi kye mu lujjudde n'endabika ye, era yalondebwa nga "Sexiest Man Alive" okuva mu People magazine mu 1991. Mu 2009, Swayze yafa kookolo w'olubuto lw'ekibumba ku myaka 57. Patrick Wayne Swayze yazaalibwa nga Agusito 18, 1952 mu Houston, Texas, omwana owookubiri owa Jesse Wayne Swayze, omukubi w'ebifaananyi, ne Patsy Swayze (aka Karnes), omuzinyi, omukubi w'ennyimba, era omusomesa w'amazina. Yalina mwannyina omukulu, Vickie, ab'oluganda abato babiri, omuzannyi Don ne Sean, ne mwannyina omuto Bambi, eyakuzibwa. Jjajja wa Swayze okuva ewa kitaawe yali Mungereza John Swayze (16191706) okuva mu Bridport mu Dorset. Mu kusenguka kw'aba Puritan mu New England wakati wa 1620 ne 1640, Swayze yatambulira ku mmeeri ya Recovery, n'atuuka mu Massachusetts Bay Colony. Yafumbirwa Katherine King okuva mu Essex era oluvannyuma yazaala abaana musanvu. Muzzukulu waabwe, Samuel, omulamuzi, y'omu ku baasooka okukozesa Siwayze spelling. Kojja we Bruce Swayze yali muzannyi wa bikonde. Swayze yakulira mu kitundu kya Oak Forest mu Houston, gyeyasomera St. Rose w'e Lima Catholic School, Frank Black Middle School era yattikirwa mu Waltrip High School ne San Jacinto College Central. Mu kaseera kano, yayiga obukugu bungi mu by'emikono n'emizannyo, nga ice skating, classical ballet, n'okuzannya emizannyo mu ssomero. Era yazannya omupiira mu ssomero lya sekendule, ng'asuubira okufuna sikaala y'omupiira mu ttendekero okutuusa lwe yafuna obuvune mu kugulu ne bukomekkereza omulimu gwe. Yalina omuwendo gwa Vietnam ogwa wansi ogwa 141, ogwayitibwa mu 1970, era yandibadde ayingizibwa, naye obuvune obw'amaanyi buyinza okuba nga bwakiremesa ekyo. Mu kiseera kye kimu yatendekebwa mu by'okulwana nga Wushu, Taekwondo, ne Aikido, bye yakozesa okutambuza obusungu bwe. Mu 1972 ku myaka 20, yasengukira mu kibuga New York okumaliriza okutendekebwa kwe mu kuzina mu masomero ga Harkness Ballet ne Joffrey Ballet. Omukubi w'ennyimba Omumerika Eliot Feld yateekateeka bbaale eri Swayze ne Mikhail Baryshanov, ekitaataaganyizibwa okuddaabiriza kw'okugulu kwa Swayze. Mu myaka gya 1980 Patrick Swayze yasookera ddala okulabikako ng'omuzinyi mu Disney Theatre Group mu pulogulaamu eyitibwa Disney on parade. Oluvannyuma yazannya ekifo kya Danny Zuko mu kimu ku bifo eby'okuddaabiriza mu muzannyo gwa Broadway ogwa Grease. Mu 1979, yakola firimu ye eyasooka nga Ace mu Skatetown, USA. Ku ntikko y'obumanyifu bwa disiko, yazannya mu Pabst Blue Ribbon commercial nga agenda ku date mu disiko-themed nightclub ne Pabst's then-jingle set to disco music. Yalabikira mu M*A*S*H episode "Blood Brothers" mu 1981 nga Private Sturgis, ng'ebiwundu bye bitono, naye nga azuuliddwa nga mulwadde nnyo ne leukemia. Yalabikira mu vidiyo ya Rosanna by Toto nga omu ku bammemba b'ekibinja. Omwaka gwegumu, yalabikira mu firimu ya terefayina eya Return of the Rebels ne Barbara Eden, era mu lukumi mu lwenda munaana, yalina akaseera akatono mu firimu ya terefayina eya The Renegades, ng'azannya omukulembeze w'ekibinja ky'abamenyi b'amateeka eyitibwa Bandit. Swayze yamanyibwa nnyo mu kisaawe kya firimu oluvanyuma lw'okuwangula ekifo kya Darel "Darry" Curtis mu firimu ya Francis Ford Coppola eya S.E. Ekitabo kya Hinton, The Outsiders, era kyagabana ku lutimbe ne abazannyi abalala nga Tom Cruise, Matt Dillon, Emilio Estevez, Leif Garrett, C. Thomas Howell, Diane Lane, Rob Lowe, ne Ralph Macchio. Mu mwaka gwe gumu, Swayze yazannya ng'omutendesi w'amagye g'oku mazzi mu firimu ya Vietnam Uncommon Valor ne Gene Hackman. Omwaka ogwaddako, Swayze, Howell, ne mukwano gwa Howell ne munne mu The Outsiders, Darren Dalton, bazzeemu okukwatagana mu firimu ya Red Dawn, wamu ne Jennifer Grey. Mu 1986, Lowe ne Swayze bazzeemu okukwatagana mu Youngblood. Obuwanguzi bwa Swayze obwasooka bwali mu firimu ya ttivvi eya 1985 eya North and South, eyateekebwawo mu kiseera ky'olutalo lw'omunda mu America. Swayze yazannya mu firimu ya 1987 eya Dirty Dancing, firimu y'embalirira entono, etegekeddwa okufulumizibwa wiiki emu yokka, oluvannyuma yagenda mu vidiyo. Swayze yazannya omutendesi w'amazina Johnny Castle wamu ne Jennifer Grey. Emboozi yasobozesa Swayze okuzina n'omukwano ne Grey n'okulaga okutendekebwa kwe mu kuzina okw'ekikugu. Ng'oggyeeko okuzannya n'okuzina, Swayze yayiiya n'okuyimba emu ku nnyimba ezaali ku doboozi lya firimu ya Dirty Dancing, "She's Like the Wind. " Oluyimba lwafuuka oluyimba lwa top 10 era okuva olwo lwayimbibwa abayimbi abalala. Swayze yali awandiise oluyimba luno ne Stacy Widelitz mu firimu ya Grandview, USA mu 1984. Ye firimu eyasooka okutunda kopi akakadde kamu ku vidiyo era, okuva mu 2009, yali efunye obukadde bwa ddoola 214 mu nsi yonna. Firimu era yafulumya enkola endala, oba ez'enjawulo, okuva ku muzannyo gwa ttivvi okutuuka ku muzannyo gwa kompyuta. Swayze yafuna okusunsulwa kw'ekirabo kya Golden Globe olw'omulimu guno. Oluvanyuma lwa Dirty Dancing, Swayze yeesanga mu bwetaavu bungi era n'alabikira mu firimu eziwerako, omuli Road House mu 1989 ne Sam Elliott, Ben Gazzara, ne Kelly Lynch. 1990s Mu firimu y'omukwano ey'enjawulo Ghost (1990), Swayze yazannya nga Sam Wheat ng'ali wamu ne Demi Moore, Whoopi Goldberg, ne Tony Goldwyn. Ghost ye firimu eyasinga okuyingiza ssente mu 1990 era ye vidiyo eyasinga okupangisibwa mu 1991. Firimu eno yasunsulwa okuwangula ekirabo kya Academy Award for Best Picture era Swayze yafuna okusunsulwa okulala okwa Golden Globes olw'okuzannya. Era Swayze yeyakakasa ba pulodyusa okupangisa Goldberg, eyebaza Swayze mu kwogera kwe okw'okusiima bweyawangula ekirabo kya Academy Award for Best Supporting Actress. Ekifo we ne Moore we bakozesa akagaali k'ebibumba kifuuse ak'amaanyi. Mu mwaka ogwaddako, yazannyira wamu ne munne mu Youngblood, Keanu Reeves mu firimu endala enkulu, Point Break, mweyakola emisomo gye egy'okubuuka mu bbanga. Magazini ya People yamutuuma erinnya lya "Sexiest Man Alive." olw'ebyo byeyakola mu kisaawe kya firimu, Swayze yaweebwa emmunyeenye ku Hollywood Walk of Fame mu 1997. Swayze yafuna obuvune mu mwezi gwokutaano 1997 bwe yali akuba firimu ya HBO eya Letters from a Killer okumpi ne Ione, California, bwe yagwa okuva ku mbalaasi n'akuba omuti. Amagulu ge gombi gaamenyeka, era n'afuna emisuwa ena mu kibegabega. Okukwata firimu kwayimirizibwa okumala emyezi ebiri. Firimu eno yafulumizibwa mu 1998, era Swayze yagenda atereera okuva ku buvune bwe, naye yafuna obuzibu okuddamu omulimu gwe okutuusa mu 2000, lwe yazannya firimu ya Forever Lulu, ne Melanie Griffith. Mu 1995, Swayze yalabikira mu firimu To Wong Foo, Thanks for Everything! Julie Newmar, ng'azannyira wamu ne Wesley Snipes ne John Leguizamo nga ba drag queens basatu nga emmotoka yaabwe eyonooneka mu lugendo lw'okusala ensi, ne babaleka nga basibidde mu kabuga akatono.
